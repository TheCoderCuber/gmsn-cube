About

Welcome to our cubing competition! We’re excited to bring together cubers from our middle schools, giving everyone a chance to test their skills and enjoy the thrill of solving. This event is a mix of newcomers and experienced cubers, so it’s the perfect opportunity to learn, compete, and make new friends in the cubing community.

We’ve set up a range of events and challenges, and we’ll have most of the essential equipment ready, including timers, cube covers, and stopwatches. We’re aiming for a fun, fair, and friendly environment, so please bring your best effort and sportsmanship!
